Our Story

The House
of Élara

Born from a desire to clothe women not in fashion, but in feeling — founded on the belief that what we wear should be both artful and enduring.

Founded 2008

A House Built on
Conviction

Maison Élara was born in a small atelier in Paris's 6th arrondissement, where our founder Élara Voss spent years working alongside master tailors before opening her own house at the age of 29.

What began as a collection of ten coats grew into a house that now dresses women across five continents. The founding principle has never changed: make something that will still be beautiful in twenty years.

We do not follow seasons. We follow feeling.

Élara Voss

Founder & Creative Director

Our Responsibility

Dressed with
Purpose

At Maison Élara, sustainability is not a feature or a campaign. It is the only way we know how to work.

From carbon-neutral shipping to living wage guarantees across the supply chain, every decision is made with the long view in mind.

  • 100% natural, traceable fibres
  • Carbon-neutral shipping on every order
  • Living wages across our entire supply chain
  • Repair programme — free for life on all garments
  • Take-back scheme: we rework, donate, or responsibly recycle
